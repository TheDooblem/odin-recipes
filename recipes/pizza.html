Pizza Recipe

Description

To make pizza. One must first roll out some pizza dough into a circle shape with a thickness of about half an inch. Then, you spread some tomato sauce on top of the pizza, leaving the edge of the pizza unsauced. Next, put some shredded cheese on top of the sauce. If you would like you can add any topping on top. Put the pizza on a metal tray and put it into a 400 degrees farenheit oven for 30 minutes. Take out the pizza when done and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • shredded cheese
  • topping of your choosing

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough into cirlce 1/2 inch thick
  2. Spread tomato sauce on top, leaving space at edge for crust
  3. Spread shredded cheese on top of sauce
  4. Add toppings of your choice
  5. Place on metal tray and put in 400 degree farenheit oven for 30 minutes
  6. Take out of oven and enjoy
