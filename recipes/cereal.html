Cereal Recipe

Description

What to eat for breakfast? It's hard to decide. What about a simple bowl of cereal and milk? But how does one make cereal and milk? Well, you've come to the right place.

Ingredients

  • cereal of your choice
  • milk of your choice

Equipment

  • spoon
  • bowl

Steps

  1. Pour cereal into your bowl
  2. Pour milk over your cereal until the milk reaches the top of the cereal
  3. Put spoon in bowl
  4. Have fun and enjoy!
