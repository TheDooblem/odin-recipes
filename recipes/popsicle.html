Popsicle Recipe

Description

Who doesn't want a nice cool popsicle on a hot day? But popsicles aren't always stocked up and they can be expensive. What if I told you you can make them at home? Well, it's true! You just need some juice, a container, and a stick. It's very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • juice of your choosing

Equipment

  • a container for the juice
  • a stick to put in juice. Like...
    • popsicle stick
    • spoon
    • metal straw
  • plastic wrap to keep stick in place
  • freezer

Steps

  1. Pour your favorite juice into a container. A drinking glass or popsicle mold could work.
  2. Place plastic wrap over the opening of container tightly
  3. Push stick through plastic wrap into container (Make sure you leave some of the stick out so you can grip it)
  4. Place container into freezer for atleast 8 hours
  5. Pull popsicle out of container and enjoy!
